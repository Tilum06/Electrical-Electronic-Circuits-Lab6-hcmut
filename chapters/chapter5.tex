\subsection{ADC Input}

The second type of the input signal is the ADC input value, which is a kind of sensing device. In this sensorpart, we use two opamps which are packed in one ICLM358. ICLM358 includes two opamps. A photoresistor (also known as a light-dependent resistor, LDR, or photo-conductive cell) is use to measure the light intensitive.
